ABOUT US

BUGC aims to help you become a glider pilot. We organise regular trips to Bristol and Gloucestershire Club (BGGC). Here you will undertake your trial and training flights, in 2-seat dual controlled gliders, under the supervision of BGA-qualified instructors.

Once you have achieved the solo standard in your training, you'll also gain access to fly our club-owned single-seat Grob glider.


TRIAL LESSONS

Fancy an introduction to the world of gliding, see how a glider flies and enjoy the stunning views? Or are you passionate about aviation and looking for your first step in training?

BUGC provides student trial flights at a discounted rate of £81. Which permits:

- A 2000ft (~20min) Aerotow Flight with an Instructor

- 3 Months of additional flying at Club Rates if you decide to come back (£10 per winch, 47p per minute)

Transport to and from the airfield is provided at £3 to cover fuel costs.

What do we use the Trial Lesson revenue for?

- Fuel/Insurance costs of your Trial Lesson's tug aircraft

- Upkeep of G-CHXM, BUGC's own aircraft

REGULAR TRAINING

For new pilots who are willing to continue dedicating their time into the sport, up to solo, BGA Bronze, Silver endorsements, CAA SPL and beyond, please notify us after your Trial Lesson and we will arrange regular training. We usually organise training every weekend (subject to weather).

If you are already a glider pilot at another airfield / is a powered pilot who would like glider conversion, email us! We are always open to pilots to fly with us during the summer season. Our club is open for all existing experience levels and can provide training beyond Solo at Nympsfield.

What is a Trial Lesson?

A Trial Lesson gives you the chance to experience the sport with an instructor before deciding if you’d like to continue training.

How long is my Trial Lesson?

Flight time is usually around 20 minutes, depending on conditions. As gliding clubs are volunteer-run, we also ask participants to help with duties on the airfield during the day.

Membership & Fees

What do I get with a BUGC Membership only?

Access to social events, group chats, and regular training (after your trial flight), plus more club benefits.

What do I get with a Trial Flight + BUGC Membership?

You get your trial flight along with full BUGC membership benefits.

Can I pay for just the Trial Flight fee without membership?

No, we require the BUGC membership as part of the base cost for a trial flight.
